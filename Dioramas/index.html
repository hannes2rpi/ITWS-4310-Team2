Late Ordovician (450 million years ago): Field Museum diorama shows Ordovician marine invertebrates in typical clear shallow waters. Models of seaweed, coral, brachiopod, clam, snail, cephalopod and trilobite. Diorama and models based on fossils discovered in Ordovician rocks in the Mississippi River Valley in Minnesota, Wisconsin, and Iowa.
